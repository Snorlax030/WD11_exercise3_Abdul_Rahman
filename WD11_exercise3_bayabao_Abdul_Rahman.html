Explore Cagayan de Oro, The city of Golden Friendship.

Cagayan de Oro City, the capital of Misamis Oriental, is a province located in Northern Mindanao. The city is called “The Whitewater Rafting Capital” of the Philippines because of the extreme sport’s popularity here. Cagayan de Oro map is also filled with other must-see spots for both thrilling and relaxing activities. It’s also the 3rd most popular destination among local tourists next to Cebu in Visayas and Davao in Mindanao, and a safe destination for foreign travelers.

If you’re looking for an adventurous getaway, we offer you this list of must-dos to add to your travel itinerary! The majority of these Cagayan de Oro tourist spots are accessible via public transportation for a convenient trip.
